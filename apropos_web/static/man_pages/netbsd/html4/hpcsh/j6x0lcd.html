NAME

j6x0lcd — driver for Jornada 680 LCD screen

SYNOPSIS

j6x0lcd* at shb?

DESCRIPTION

The j6x0lcd driver provides support for controlling brightness, contrast, and power of the LCD screen in Jornada 680 series machines.

The default keymap binds <Ctrl> + <Alt> + <arrowkey> to control brightness with up and down arrow keys, and to control contrast with left and right arrow keys.

SEE ALSO

screenblank(1)

HISTORY

The j6x0lcd driver first appeared in NetBSD 2.0.
