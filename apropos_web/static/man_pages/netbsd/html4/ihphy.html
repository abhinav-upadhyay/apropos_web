NAME

ihphy — Driver for Intel “Hanksville” i82577 10/100/1000 Ethernet PHYs

SYNOPSIS

ihphy* at mii? phy ?

DESCRIPTION

The ihphy driver supports the Intel i82577 10/100/1000 Ethernet PHYs. These PHYs are found on the Lenovo T510 and Thinkpad X201i.
