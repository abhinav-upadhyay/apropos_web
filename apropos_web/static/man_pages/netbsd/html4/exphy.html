NAME

exphy — Driver for 3Com 3c905B-TX internal Ethernet PHY

SYNOPSIS

exphy* at mii? phy ?

DESCRIPTION

The exphy driver supports the internal PHY on 3Com 3c905B-TX 10/100 Ethernet interfaces.
