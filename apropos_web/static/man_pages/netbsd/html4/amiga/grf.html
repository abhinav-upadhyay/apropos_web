NAME

grf — Amiga graphics frame buffer device

SYNOPSIS

grf0 at grfcc0
grf1 at grfrt0
grf2 at grfrh0
grf3 at grfcl?
grf4 at grful0
grf5 at grfcv0
grf6 at grfet?
grf7 at grfcv3d0

DESCRIPTION

This is frame buffer device for Amiga. There is fixed mapping to the common video hardware. If the device is configured, the frame buffers are mapped as follows:
grf0
custom chips
grf1 & grf2
Retina Z2/Z3
grf3
Cirrus boards
grf4
A2410 video
grf5
CyberVision 64
grf6
ET4000 boards (Tseng - oMniBus, Domino, Merlin)
grf7
CyberVision 64/3D
