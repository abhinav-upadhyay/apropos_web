NAME

iso646.h - alternative spellings

SYNOPSIS

#include <iso646.h>

DESCRIPTION

The <iso646.h> header shall define the following eleven macros (on the left) that expand to the corresponding tokens (on the right):
and
&&
and_eq
&=
bitand
&
bitor
|
compl
~
not
!
not_eq
!=
or
||
or_eq
|=
xor
^
xor_eq
^=
 
The following sections are informative.
