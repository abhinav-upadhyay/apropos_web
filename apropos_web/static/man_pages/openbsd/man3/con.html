NAME

conj, conjf, conjl — compute the complex conjugate

SYNOPSIS

#include <complex.h>
double complex
conj(double complex z);
float complex
conjf(float complex z);
long double complex
conjl(long double complex z);

DESCRIPTION

The conj(), conjf() and conjl() functions reverse the sign of the imaginary part of z, producing the complex conjugate.

RETURN VALUES

The conj(), conjf() and conjl() functions return the complex conjugate of the complex number z.

SEE ALSO

carg(3)

STANDARDS

The conj(), conjf() and conjl() functions conform to ISO/IEC 9899:1999 (“ISO C99”).
