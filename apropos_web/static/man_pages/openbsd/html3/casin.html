NAME

casinh, casinhf, casinhl — complex inverse hyperbolic sine

SYNOPSIS

#include <complex.h>
double complex
casinh(double complex z);
float complex
casinhf(float complex z);
long double complex
casinhl(long double complex z);

DESCRIPTION

The casinh(), casinhf() and casinhl() functions compute the complex inverse hyperbolic sine of z.
For all complex floating-point numbers z,
casinh(z) = -i casin(iz).

RETURN VALUES

The casinh(), casinhf() and casinhl() functions return the complex inverse hyperbolic sine of z with imaginary part in the interval [-Pi/2, Pi/2], and unbounded real part.

SEE ALSO

cacosh(3), catanh(3)

STANDARDS

The casinh(), casinhf() and casinhl() functions conform to ISO/IEC 9899:1999 (“ISO C99”).
