NAME

asbtm — Asus ASB 100 temperature sensor

SYNOPSIS

asbtm* at iic?

DESCRIPTION

The asbtm driver supports the Asus ASB 100 temperature sensor, making its sensor readings available through a sysctl(8) interface.
The driver reads seven voltage (A/D) inputs, three fan inputs, two on-chip temperature sensors and up to two satellite temperature sensors.

SEE ALSO

iic(4), intro(4), sensorsd(8), sysctl(8)
