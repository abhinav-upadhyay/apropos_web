NAME

nsphyter — National Semiconductor DP83815/DP83843/DP83847 10/100 Ethernet PHY

SYNOPSIS

nsphyter* at mii?

DESCRIPTION

The nsphyter driver supports the National Semiconductor PHYTER DP83843/DP83847 Ethernet PHYs. This driver also supports the integrated PHY on the MacPHYTER DP83815. These PHYs are found on a variety of high-performance Ethernet interfaces.
