NAME

admtm — Analog Devices ADM1025 temperature and voltage sensor

SYNOPSIS

admtm* at iic?

DESCRIPTION

The admtm driver provides support for the Analog Devices ADM1025, SMSC 47m192, and Philips NE1619 temperature sensor. The device possesses internal and external temperature sensors, plus a variety of voltage sensors. These values are made available through the sysctl(8) interface.

SEE ALSO

iic(4), intro(4), sensorsd(8), sysctl(8)

HISTORY

The admtm driver first appeared in OpenBSD 3.9.

AUTHORS

The admtm driver was written by Theo de Raadt <deraadt@openbsd.org>.
