NAME

uberry — Research In Motion BlackBerry

SYNOPSIS

uberry* at uhub?

DESCRIPTION

The uberry driver provides the most minimal support for the USB-connected Research In Motion BlackBerry devices, permitting them to be charged and powered from a USB port.

SEE ALSO

uhub(4), usb(4)

HISTORY

The uberry driver first appeared in OpenBSD 4.1.

BUGS

The uberry driver is not able to charge a 7750 (USB cradle model) which has a completely dead battery, and we do not know why.
