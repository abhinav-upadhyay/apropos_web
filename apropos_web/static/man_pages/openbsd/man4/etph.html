NAME

etphy — Agere/LSI ET1011 TruePHY Gigabit Ethernet PHY

SYNOPSIS

etphy* at mii?

DESCRIPTION

The etphy driver supports the Agere/LSI ET1011 TruePHY 10/100/1000 Ethernet PHYs including the integrated TruePHY in ET1310 based adapters.

SEE ALSO

ifmedia(4), intro(4), mii(4), ifconfig(8)

HISTORY

The etphy device driver first appeared in OpenBSD 4.3.

AUTHORS

The etphy driver was written by Sepherosa Ziehau for DragonFly and ported to OpenBSD by Jonathan Gray <jsg@openbsd.org>.
