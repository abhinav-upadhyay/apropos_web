NAME

auglx — AMD Geode LX CS5536 integrated AC'97 audio device

SYNOPSIS

auglx* at pci?
audio* at auglx?

DESCRIPTION

The auglx device driver supports the integrated AC'97 audio controller of the AMD Geode LX CS5536 companion chip.

SEE ALSO

ac97(4), audio(4), intro(4), pci(4)

HISTORY

The auglx device driver first appeared in OpenBSD 4.4.
